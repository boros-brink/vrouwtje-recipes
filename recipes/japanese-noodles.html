Japanese Noodles

Let's get started

This is a noodles dish that's fairly easy and quick to make. It's Japanese-style made the traditional but nonetheless easy way, for the culture lovers here.

You could try other meat with this, or adjust the amount of certain igredients for example to your own liking. Just try things out and see what you like best!

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles
  • Cooked ham
  • Spoon of oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare the noodles according to the instructions on the packaging
  2. Cut the ham in little pieces
  3. Bake the eggs with a spoon of oil
  4. Mix everything together and enjoy your meal
